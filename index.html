Should You Build Your Own PC?

Building a personal computer, or PC, from scratch is a regular practice done by individuals interested in customizing their machines to their likings. This is done as opposed to purchasing a prebuilt PC, which are computers already assembled by their manufacturers. Before committing to your technological journey, assess your interests. Does building a PC suit your interests? Do you find the potential drawbacks negligible?

Benefits to Building a PC:

  • More customization
  • Cost-efficient
  • Better cooling
